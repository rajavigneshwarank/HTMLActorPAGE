Surya

Saravanan Sivakumar (born 23 July 1975), known professionally as Suriya, is an Indian actor and film producer who predominantly works in Tamil cinema. Suriya is considered as one of the most versatile[4][5] and highest paid Tamil actor.[6][7] He has received numerous accolades including two National Film Awards,[8] six Filmfare Awards South and five Tamil Nadu State Film Awards.[9] Suriya has featured six times in the Celebrity 100 list of Forbes India, which takes into account the earnings of Indian celebrities.
